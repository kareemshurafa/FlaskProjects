Frequently Asked Questions
Why should I use Koffing? +

Koffing is a web application designed to provide dynamic logging of asthma sufferers, up-to-date information about air quality and pollution, and further details such as what to do in emergencies.

How can I use Koffing? +

There are currently 2 main features available on Koffing
1. Personal Asthma logging
2. Air Quality Information

How often do I need to renew my inhaler +

There are two types of inhalers: relievers (relieve symptoms when occur) and preventers (prevent symptoms from developing). How often you need to use your inhaler depends on your condition, and how long your inhaler should last depends on how often you use it as well as, how many doses was in the new inhale

How does Air pollution affect asthma patients? +

Air pollution is a significant danger to people with asthma, and living in an area that has low pollution can change their life experience. Pollutants in the air can irritate and restrict airway causing breathing problems. Both short-term and long-term exposure can cause health problems such as lung function and asthma effects. Asthma is a lung condition that makes it difficult for patients to breathe comfortabily. Please refer to our Asthma Information page for more information.
